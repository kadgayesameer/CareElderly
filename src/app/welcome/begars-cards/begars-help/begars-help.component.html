Help Dr. Abhijit Treat Beggars for Free.

Dr. Abhijit Sonawane and his wife Dr. Manisha Sonawane are working together to bring medicine to the most deprived group of individuals in Pune, India. Under their organization, Soham Trust, these doctors for beggars treat individuals that do not have financial means for medical treatments. “We provide all the basic health care and investigations on [the] road itself to the elder beggars, who are abandoned by their families and who are begging just to survive in this world,” says Dr. Abhijit.

Pune based NGO starts Anti-Beggar Awareness Campaign in Mumbai

A Pune-based Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO), Suraksha has begun an anti-beggar drive at the Mumbai Central railway station. It started its campaign from Pune and has also held demonstrations in Mumbai’s Dadar and Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus railway stations.

This Man Is Fighting Against the System to Give Beggars a Dignified Life.

Sharad Patel, a farmer’s son from Mirzapur in the Hardoi district of Uttar Pradesh, was supposed to acquire a degree and settle for a well-paying job to support his family. However, he took a different path and decided to help thousands of homeless people who are forced to beg on the streets of Lucknow..
